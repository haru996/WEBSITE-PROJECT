WHY COMPUTING & CREATIVE MEDIA

As we advance further in a hyper-connected world, digital literacy and technological knowledge becomes more of a necessity than a simple advantage. Technologies that once aided us in our daily activities are now designed to mimic the way we think, imagine and create. It is through the linkage of business, creativity and technology that we may continue to transform untapped creative assets into resources that will spur economic growth, job creation and export earnings. Utilising a combination of strategic industry collaborations and academic partnerships, the Computing and Creative Media programmes at KDU produce young talents that not only fulfil the needs of the industry, but help shape what it becomes in the future. By merging creativity with technology, KDU promotes technology artistry and produce graduates that will be innovation leaders.


REAL WORLD CHALLENGES

Be taught by subject matter experts and industry practitioners who bring industry practices and methodologies into the classroom. Our philosophy is to nurture the development of talents who will establish themselves as the future of computing and game development.


SYLLABUS ENDORSED BY MAJOR GAME DEVELOPMENT STUDIOS

With a streamlined syllabus designed for producing sought after graduates in this field, KDU also spearheads the MyGameDev talent development initiative, a National Key Economic Area (NKEA) to create a high-income and sustainable creative economy in the area of Game Development – a Malaysian first.


THE MOST ADVANCED COMPUTING LABS

With leading technologies, infrastructure, software and hardware, KDU is the place to learn, research and explore. We are the only private institution with fully-equipped Alienware game development studios.
